Changing your Honda’s oil at the right time will help to keep your vehicle running smoothly. To help get the most out of your Honda, it has been equipped with a Maintenance Minder system. The Maintenance Minder tracks the intervals between oil changes based on your driving habits. Once the“oil life” indicator reaches 15%, you’ll be notified each time you start the vehicle that you shoul consider changing the vehicle’s oil. It’s recommended that you have the oil changed by your local Honda dealership before the indicator reaches 0% to ensure the best performance. If you have an older model Honda that does not come with a Maintenance Minder, you should follow the maintenance schedule included in your Owner’s Manual.
